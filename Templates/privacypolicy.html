5. Cookies

Cookies allow the website to remember crucial information, making your visit more convenient. A "web beacon" is a little object or picture that is used to track activity on a web page, application, or email. They're also known as pixels and tags. KGD Cafe, like most websites, uses cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies to improve your online experience.
